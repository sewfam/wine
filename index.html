Welcome to Seward Family Vineyard

Located in the scenic Alexander Valley of Healdsburg, California, our family-owned vineyard has been producing exceptional Cabernet Sauvignon grapes since 2002.

Our Story

We combine traditional techniques with modern practices to grow great grapes that reflect the unique terroir of the Alexander Valley.

Our vineyard's fertile soil and ideal climate allow us to grow grapes of the highest quality, which we also proudly offer to fellow winemakers and grape enthusiasts. Every grape cluster embodies the rich heritage and distinctive character of our region.

Visit Us

In 2008, we made our first barrel of wine. You can't buy it, but you can come visit us to try it.
