Welcome to Seward Family Wines

Located in the scenic Alexander Valley of Healdsburg, California, our family-owned winery has been crafting exceptional Cabernet Sauvignon since 2008.

Our Story

Founded by Albert Lee Seward III, our winery was born out of a passion for winemaking and a dedication to quality. We combine traditional techniques with modern practices to produce wines that reflect the unique terroir of Sonoma County.

Our Vineyard

Our vineyard benefits from the fertile soil and ideal climate of Alexander Valley, allowing us to cultivate premium Cabernet Sauvignon grapes. Our commitment to sustainable farming ensures the highest quality in every bottle.

Visit Us

We invite you to visit our tasting room, where you can enjoy our latest releases and experience the beauty of our vineyard. Our knowledgeable staff will guide you through a tasting of our award-winning wines.
